The Water Radical: 氵
The water radical 氵appears usually on the left side in a kanji.
Examples:
酒 (さけ) - sake
海 (うみ) - sea / ocean
湖 (みずうみ) - lake
河 (かわ) - river
浜 (はま) - seacoast / beach
池 (いけ) - pond
